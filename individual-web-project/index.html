PHASE 1

AVENGERS ASSEMBLE

Iron Man- Billionaire arms-dealing playboy Tony Stark has a life-changing experience in the desert and builds himself a hero suit. He refines the suit and uses it to combat crime and terrorism. He meets Natasha Romanoff, aka Black Widow, and her SHIELD boss Nick Fury.

The Incredible Hulk- Earnest science guy Bruce Banner has a life-changing experience in the lab and becomes a confused, angry green giant.

Iron Man 2- With the world now aware that he is Iron Man, billionaire inventor Tony Stark faces pressure from all sides to share his technology with the military. He is reluctant to divulge the secrets of his armored suit, fearing the information will fall into the wrong hands.

Thor- Alien god-type guy Thor is exiled to Earth and learns about sacrifice in a life-changing experience before defeating some frost giants.

Captain America: The First Avenger- Steve Rogers has a life-changing serum pumped into him and becomes a Hydra-bashing demigod in blue tights, then gets frozen for 70-odd years before thawing out in 2011.

The Avengers- Together they all join forces with Hawkeye under Nick Fury's aegis to take down Thor's naughty half-brother Loki and his alien hordes.

PHASE 2

ON YOUR LEFT

Iron Man 3- A traumatized Tony Stark steps back from the action, only to be drawn back into it by another tech-industry figure and his puppet villain, an actor called Trevor who was the toast of Croydon.

Thor: The Dark World- Thor has some trouble with some Dark Elves.

Captain America: The Winter Soldier- Captain America is forced to turn against his own corrupt government when his old pal Bucky turns up as a brainwashed supersoldier.

Guardians of the Galaxy- Peter Quill is forced into an uneasy truce with four disparate misfits: gun-toting Rocket Raccoon, treelike-humanoid Groot, enigmatic Gamora, and vengeance-driven Drax the Destroyer to save the universe.

Avengers: Age of Ultron- The Avengers team up again to take down the cyber-consciousness Ultron, wrecking the country of Sokovia in the process.

PHASE 3

I AM INEVITABLE

Captain America: Civil War- Captain America believes superheroes should remain free to defend humanity without government interference. Iron Man sharply disagrees and supports oversight. Avengers must choose a side.

Doctor Strange- Dr. Stephen Strange's life changes after a car accident robs him of the use of his hands.

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol 2- As the Guardians try to escape the Sovereign after double crossing them, the mystery of Peter's parentage is revealed.

Spider-Man: Homecoming- Under the watchful eye of mentor Tony Stark, Parker starts to embrace his newfound identity as Spider-Man.

Thor: Ragnarok- Imprisoned on the other side of the universe, the mighty Thor finds himself in a deadly gladiatorial contest that pits him against the Hulk, his former ally and fellow Avenger. Thor's quest for survival leads him in a race against time to prevent the all-powerful Hela from destroying his home world and the Asgardian civilization.

Black Panther- After the death of his father, T'Challa returns home to the African nation of Wakanda to take his rightful place as king. When a powerful enemy suddenly reappears, T'Challa's mettle as king and as Black Panther gets tested.

Avengers: Infinity War- Iron Man, Thor, the Hulk and the rest of the Avengers unite to battle their most powerful enemy yet -- the evil Thanos.

Ant-Man and the Wasp- Scott Lang is grappling with the consequences of his choices as both a superhero and a father. Approached by Hope van Dyne and Dr. Hank Pym, Lang must once again don the Ant-Man suit and fight alongside the Wasp.

Captain Marvel- Set in 1995, Captain Marvel is an extraterrestrial Kree warrior who finds herself caught in the middle of an intergalactic battle between her people and the Skrulls.

Avengers: Endgame- The remaining Avengers -- Iron Man, Thor, Black Widow, Captain America, Hawkeye and Bruce Banner -- must figure out a way to bring back their vanquished allies for an epic showdown with Thanos

Spider-Man Far From Home- Parker soon finds himself donning the Spider-Man suit to help Fury and fellow superhero Mysterio stop evil entities from wreaking havoc across the Europe.
